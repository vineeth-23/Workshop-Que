Scenario card

One System, Many Stories

A customer calls after discovering a reconnection fee on their bill. They have already tried a chatbot and failed to get help. The automated phone system misunderstands them more than once. When they finally reach a live agent, the agent wants to help, but the screen in front of them shows that the fee is valid and that the customer is not eligible for a full waiver.

The agent is also being measured on speed, compliance, and call length. The point of the exercise is that the AI system is not just one thing. It is the chatbot, the routing logic, the score, the dashboard, the script, the waiver rules, and the management metrics around the human agent.
